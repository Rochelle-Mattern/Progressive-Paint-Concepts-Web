About Us

Founded in 1979 by Todd Mattern, Progressive Paint Concepts is San Diego’s #1 choice for paint contractors. We specialize in residential, industrial, commercial, and HOA painting.



In addition to interior and exterior painting our services include:


  • Drywall repair
  • Acoustical removal and texture
  • Crown molding installation
  • Baseboard installation
  • Stucco Repair
  • Wood repair (non-structural)
  • Wall texturing
  • Complete surface preparation
  • Expert color consolation
  • Faux finishes (and more)


We are proud that the majority of our new clients are referrals. You will not regret hiring us! Providing tier-one customer service is a top priority, we ensure quality and satisfaction.

For all your painting needs, call us or email us now to request a free, no-obligation professional consultation.
